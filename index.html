Need to Know

FAQ

How are pieces sourced and graded?
Every item is hand-selected from verified warehouses, estate sales, and trusted wholesale sources. Each piece is graded on a scale of Excellent, Very Good, or Good — condition notes are always listed on the product page.
Are designer finds authenticated?
Yes. All designer and premium-label items go through a two-step authentication check by our in-house team. Suspect listings are rejected before they hit the site — always.
How does sizing work on vintage pieces?
Vintage sizing runs differently to modern cuts. We include flat measurements (pit-to-pit, length, waist, rise) on every single listing. When in doubt, measure first. We also include a modern size equivalent as a rough guide.
What are the shipping and delivery times?
Standard shipping: 3–6 business days. Express: 1–2 days. International varies by destination. Free standard shipping on all orders over $75. Orders are processed within 24 hours of payment confirmation.
What is your returns policy?
Returns accepted within 14 days of delivery on items that don't match their listed condition. If there's an error on our end, return shipping is covered. Items marked as final sale are non-returnable.
How is Thrift to Drip sustainable?
Every purchase extends a garment's life by years — that's the core. We also use recycled poly mailers and carbon-offset shipping options. Resale is the most sustainable fashion choice you can make.
